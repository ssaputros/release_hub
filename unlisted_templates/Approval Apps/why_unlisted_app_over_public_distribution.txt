We prefer unlisted app distribution over public distribution on the App Store because it ensures the app is accessible exclusively to our intended audience, which includes employees and authorized personnel. This approach minimizes the risk of unauthorized access and protects sensitive organizational data, which is critical given the nature of the app’s functionality and content. Unlisted distribution also aligns better with our use case, as the app is designed specifically for internal purposes and is not intended for the general public.

Additionally, unlisted distribution simplifies deployment and management by allowing us to share the app through direct links or controlled internal channels. This avoids the challenges associated with public listings, such as the potential for confusion among external users or the need to handle external reviews and feedback. By focusing solely on the intended user base, unlisted distribution enhances privacy, ensures compliance with organizational policies, and allows us to maintain greater control over the app's accessibility and usage.
