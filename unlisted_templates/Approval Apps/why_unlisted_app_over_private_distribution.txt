We prefer unlisted app distribution over private distribution via Apple Business Manager or Apple School Manager because it offers a simpler and more flexible method of sharing the app with our client’s specific organization. Unlisted distribution allows us to directly share the app via a unique link, ensuring it is easily accessible to the intended users without requiring additional setup or management within Apple's Business or School Manager systems.

While private distribution through Apple Business Manager or Apple School Manager is useful for large-scale enterprise distribution, it involves a more complex process of managing and configuring user access, which may not be necessary for our use case. The app is tailored specifically for one organization, and unlisted distribution provides a more streamlined approach, avoiding the administrative overhead associated with configuring and managing multiple organizations or accounts.

Additionally, unlisted distribution provides more direct control over who can access the app, allowing us to keep it exclusive to the intended users while maintaining the privacy and security of the app. This method suits our needs for a single organization without the need for complex enterprise distribution systems.
